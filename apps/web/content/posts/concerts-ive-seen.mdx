I started keeping a list after I realised I'd already forgotten half of them. Concerts are weird that way — you're fully there, and then they're gone. This is my attempt to hold onto a few before they fade.

**Aha! — NOS Alive, Lisbon • 2024**
The tent was overflowing and everyone was screaming every word back at Joe. "Never Fight a Man with a Perm" turned five thousand strangers into a gang for forty minutes.

**The Strokes — NOS Alive, Lisbon • 2024**
Julian Casablancas showed up twenty minutes late, mumbled through "Last Nite," and it was still the most charismatic thing I've ever watched. Some bands are too cool to try and that's part of the appeal.

**Arctic Monkeys — NOS Alive, Lisbon • 2023**
The Tranquility Base era live. Polarising setlist but "Body Paint" live is undeniable — the outro builds into something that studios can't capture.
